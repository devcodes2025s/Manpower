Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can you provide staff?

For temporary staffing, we can often provide qualified candidates within 24-48 hours. Permanent and executive placements typically take 2-4 weeks depending on the role's complexity and requirements.

What industries do you specialize in?

We serve multiple industries including Manufacturing, Healthcare, IT & Technology, Retail, Hospitality, Construction, Finance, and Logistics. Our recruiters have specialized knowledge in each sector.

How do you screen candidates?

Our rigorous screening process includes skills assessments, background checks, reference verification, behavioral interviews, and industry-specific evaluations to ensure quality matches.

What are your fees?

Our fees vary based on the service type and position level. We offer competitive pricing and flexible payment structures. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific needs.

Do you offer replacement guarantees?

Yes! We offer a 90-day replacement guarantee for permanent placements. If a candidate doesn't work out within this period, we'll find a replacement at no additional charge.

Can I convert temporary staff to permanent?

Absolutely! We offer temp-to-perm conversion options. This allows you to evaluate candidates in real work situations before making a permanent hiring decision.
